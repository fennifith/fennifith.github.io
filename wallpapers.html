Fornax

Free wallpapers, made by everyone, for everyone.

When posting to a website, credit is required for most wallpapers. For credit to be given, you must include their name and a link to their profile page or website. These can be found in the about section. Please contact the designer before using any wallpapers or illustrations in another app.
As with all my other current applications, this app is and will remain ad-free and with no requests for donations or "pay to unlock this feature" popups. All I ask is that you provide some feedback about what I can do to improve your experience with this app.
