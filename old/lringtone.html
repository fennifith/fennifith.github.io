Location Ringtone changes your ringtone based on your location.

This app automatically changes your ringtone based on preset locations, so that you don't have to put your phone on vibrate every time you get to work, or on silent at the library, or on loud at your favorite restaurant.
As with all my other current applications, this app is and will remain ad-free and with no requests for donations or "pay to unlock this feature" popups. All I ask is that you provide some feedback about what I can do to improve your experience with this app.
